Vascular VR

Vascular VR is a cross-platform VR application meant to bring state-of-the-art reconstruction technology to non-specialist physicians. By using stereoscopic rendering, natural navigation techniques, and proprioception-enhanced memory - Vascular VR allows clinicians to tweak and tune rapid prototype models created with the assitance of state-of-the-art algorithms, without the need for time-consuming requests of uniquely trained engineers to perform the alterations.

While orginally invisioned as a stand-alone piece of software, SurfLab has since converted the software to be built on top of the unity platform. started the instantiation of an accompanying Python script to be used in Blender that will rig the blood vessel and tumor models with appropriate spring constraints. These rigged models are then used in another one of our lab's projects, TIPS, which is seen in proof-of-concept at the end of the embedded demo of the below presentation.
